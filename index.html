    Welcome to HAG website.

    The Historical Address Geocoder(HAG-GIS)is a Python package for automating the geocoding process for the Digitising Scotland project. The geocoding process will involve probability-matching historical records with contemporary addresses, designing an automating system to assist manual matching of historical addresses, and producing a small area geography from historical addresses. 

     A Beta version of HAG package will be available by the end of December 2014.
